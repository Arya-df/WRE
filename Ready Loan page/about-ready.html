ABOUT WINDERMERE READY

Windermere Ready provides home sellers with loans of up to $50,000 (up to $100,000 in select states) to cover repairs, upgrades, and staging. By investing in improvements before listing, sellers can enhance their home’s appeal and potentially achieve a higher sale price. Your Windermere agent will work with you to create a targeted plan that focuses on the projects with the highest return on investment.

Why work with Windermere Ready:

  • Upgrade now, pay later — no payments are due until your home sells**
  • Flexible contractor choice — select licensed professionals of your choice for repairs and upgrades
  • Fast, simple process — quick application, approval, and funding to keep your timeline on track
  • Maximize home value — invest in strategic improvements like painting, landscaping, staging, and repairs
  • Expert guidance — your Windermere agent helps prioritize projects with the best ROI for your home
