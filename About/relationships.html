a legacy of relationships

In 1972, John Jacobi founded Windermere with a simple belief: real estate should be about more than transactions—it should be about relationships, integrity, and community. Starting with a single office in Seattle's Windermere neighborhood, he built a company where collaboration mattered more than competition and putting people first was the foundation for success.

Over the decades, John's children and son-in-law carried that vision forward, guiding Windermere's growth while preserving the values that shaped its culture. Today, a third generation of the Jacobi family is helping lead the company into the future—honoring John's original vision while continuing to evolve for the next generation.

More than 50 years later, Windermere remains family-owned, community-focused, and committed to the belief that relationships will always come first.
